Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 22, 2026

MVS Solves is committed to making mvssolves.com usable by as many people as possible, including people with disabilities.

1. Our Approach

We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA where practical, including: sufficient color contrast, visible keyboard focus states, semantic heading structure, alt text on meaningful images, and support for reduced-motion preferences (animations are disabled site-wide when your system requests reduced motion).

2. Known Limitations

This site uses custom motion and visual effects (scroll animations, a background particle canvas, custom navigation). We test these against keyboard and screen-reader use, but as a small, independently operated business we cannot guarantee full conformance at all times. If you hit a barrier, we want to know — see Section 4.

3. Ongoing Work

Accessibility is treated as an ongoing responsibility, not a one-time fix. We review and improve this site's accessibility as it evolves.

4. Feedback and Contact

If you experience any accessibility barrier using this site, please tell us — we want to fix it. Contact MVS Solves at martin@mvssolves.com, and include the page and a description of the issue. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
