Careers — Closer

We don't hire salespeople. We hire closers.

The Role

What you do. What's required.

The Work
  • Free training first — weekly, ongoing, not a one-off course. Earn from client one — first five guided, then you're live, running your own leads
  • Find your own on top if you want more — templates provided, no ceiling
  • A lead is a contact, nothing more — no website built until signed and paid
  • Once it's yours, it stays yours. No interference, no CRM, no admin
The Bar
  • 20 hours a week, minimum. Not clocked — delivered
  • Remote, any country, any timezone — you respond in minutes, not "when you get to it"
  • You listen before you talk, and follow up every time without being told twice
  • Self-motivated, no experience required — lying about it ends things fast
Commission

No base. No salary. No cap.

You earn on what you close. Nothing else. One ladder, same for everyone.

Monthly Commission
10% → 20% → 30%

Recurring clients 1–5: 10%. 6–10: 20%. 11+: 30% — and flat from there on.

Outright Commission
30% flat, always

One-time deals pay 30% — client 1 or client 100, no ladder, no low tier, no high tier.

  • Recurring commission runs for the lifetime of the client's payments — no cutoff
  • Paid monthly, within 7 days of payment actually clearing
  • One-time deals pay their flat 30% once, at close — and don't count toward your recurring tier or the bonus ladder below. Only recurring clients build toward those
  • You get paid in whatever currency the client actually pays in — no conversion, no cut, wherever you both are in the world

Cross a recurring tier threshold and only new recurring deals earn the higher rate — what you've already closed keeps its original rate.

Milestone Bonuses

Commission isn't the ceiling.

Recurring clients closed — one-time deals don't count, same rule as the tiers above. Stacks on top of whatever commission you're already earning.

50 Clients
+$1,000a month, on top of commission

Not a bonus. Not once. Every month you're still active.

75 Clients
$3,000once-off, on top of the monthly bonus

A lump sum, on top of the $1,000/month that's already running.

100 Clients
$5,000once-off, stacked on the monthly bonus

Another lump sum, still stacked on top of the $1,000/month.

Bonus figures are always paid in USD, wherever you're based — a fixed number, not a local conversion. This is different from commission, which is paid in whichever currency the client themselves pays in.

Standards slip, you're out — no review cycle, no warning system. Miss the bar (hours, follow-up, results) and the contract ends immediately. No notice period. No second conversation.

Apply

This is not for everyone.

If you cannot commit 20 hours a week, do not apply. If you've never closed anything, say so — plainly, not padded. State your experience, your availability, and why you think you're worth the risk. No CV needed.
