MVS Solves is a one-person agency that's outgrowing "one person." I need someone who can take warm, qualified leads and turn them into signed clients — without me on every call.

What MVS Solves does

Websites, booking systems & automation for service businesses.

I build and run the digital basics local service businesses need — websites, online booking, Google visibility, automations, AI assistants — priced simply, delivered fast, no agency layers. Every lead that reaches you has already shown real interest.

The role

What a closer does here

  • Work warm leads from my own outreach and inbound — you're not cold-prospecting from zero
  • Get on calls or WhatsApp, understand what the business needs, and close the deal
  • Once a lead is assigned to you, it's yours — I stay out of it and let you run the conversation
  • Report back on what closed, what didn't, and why
Who fits

What I'm looking for

  • You can hold a real conversation and actually listen — not read a script
  • You follow up. Every time. Without being told to
  • You're comfortable closing over a call or WhatsApp, no fancy CRM required
  • Self-motivated — you don't need someone checking in on you daily
Pay

Commission-based

No base salary, no fixed hours — this suits someone who wants to be paid on results, not present hours.
