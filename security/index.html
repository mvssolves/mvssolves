Security

Last updated: July 22, 2026

MVS Solves handles client data and, where a Service involves it, your end-customers' data. Here's how that's protected.

1. Infrastructure

Websites and services built by MVS Solves run on established, reputable third-party infrastructure (hosting, payments, scheduling, and automation platforms) rather than self-hosted, unmanaged servers — so the underlying platform security, patching, and uptime are handled by providers whose full-time job that is.

2. Encryption in Transit

Sites and services built by MVS Solves are served over HTTPS. Data submitted through forms, bookings, or payments is encrypted in transit.

3. Payments

Payments are handled entirely by a third-party payment processor. MVS Solves does not directly collect or store full card numbers on its own systems.

4. Access Control

Access to client systems, hosting accounts, and business tools is limited to what's needed to deliver the Services, and credentials are not shared or reused across unrelated systems.

5. Data Minimization

MVS Solves processes end-customer personal information (where applicable, per the Data Processing Agreement) only as needed to operate the Service you've purchased — not for our own separate use.

6. Reporting a Security Issue

If you believe you've found a security vulnerability affecting mvssolves.com or a site/service built by MVS Solves, please report it responsibly: email martin@mvssolves.com with details and steps to reproduce. Please do not publicly disclose an issue before we've had a reasonable chance to address it.

7. Related Policies

See also the Privacy Policy, Data Processing Agreement, and Terms of Service.
